The Human Tech Project

The Human Tech Project is a student-led 501(c)(3) nonprofit that aims to find solutions to global problems with technically advanced and impactful projects. We follow an intricate 4-step process to ensure these projects truly make an impact: Market Analysis, R&D and Prototyping, Outreach and Implementation, and Expansion. We supplement these initiatives with coding and hardware classes for children, with the goal of fostering creativity and a passion for technology, preparing the generation of tomorrow to continue what we started.
